El Pollastre

Here we are, with my most obscure game ever. El Pollastre.

A little bit of context.

So I had this one friend, which I will call ElPoyo (like Pollo, chicken is Spanish, but badly wroten) and I had some fights with him like 5 or 6 months earlier, the thing is, that I wanted to become friends again, but he didn't. So I thought "maybe if I make a videogame for him, he will forgive me!" little did I know that it was going to be USELESS.

Screenshots from the only level and build existing of the game, dated on June 2023.

There's not a lot to say about this game, as it was in a very early state when I stopped it. I was going to publish it with 5 levels.
Mountain, Desert, City, Gym, and the ending, The Cave.
Where El Pollastre would've had to save his girlfriend, La Polla.
Apart from the first level, the only thing I have is the second level background, which was later repurposed as the main background for Cactus Guy.
